Service Times & Schedule

Join us for worship, prayer, and fellowship

Regular Weekly Services

Sunday Service

Sunday 10:00 AM

Our main weekly worship service featuring sermon, hymns, and Holy Communion. All are welcome to join us for spiritual nourishment and community fellowship.

Service starts promptly at 10:00 AM

Seeker Service

Wednesday 9:00 AM

Morning devotion and prayer service for spiritual seekers. A time for personal reflection, scripture reading, and morning prayers.

Mercy Day Service

Wednesday 6:00 PM

Mid-week spiritual renewal service focusing on God's mercy and grace. Includes prayers, testimonies, and special intercessions.

Monthly Services

New Moon Service

When: First Thursday of every month
Time: 11:00 PM
Purpose: Spiritual cleansing, renewal, and prayers for the new month

The New Moon Service is a special monthly observance in the Celestial Church of Christ tradition. It's a time for spiritual cleansing, thanksgiving, and prayers for guidance in the coming month.

Service Guidelines

Punctuality

Please arrive 10-15 minutes before service begins. Sunday service starts promptly at 10:00 AM.

All Are Welcome

Everyone is welcome regardless of background. We are a community of believers open to all.

Dress Code

Modest and respectful attire is recommended. White garments are traditional but not required.

Children

Children are welcome in our services. We have special provisions for young worshippers.
