Building Project

Building a Better Worship Space for Our Community

Support Our Church Building Project

Our church is currently undertaking an important building project to create a better worship space for our growing community. This project will provide:

  • Expanded seating capacity for our congregation
  • Modern facilities for youth programs
  • Improved accessibility for all members
  • Enhanced audio-visual equipment for services
  • Community meeting spaces
  • Parking facilities for visitors

We need the support of our members and well-wishers to make this vision a reality. Every contribution brings us closer to completing this important project.
