About Our Church

Our history, mission, and beliefs

Our History

The Celestial Church of Christ Redemption Parish in Winnipeg was established to serve the spiritual needs of the Celestial Church community in Manitoba. We are committed to spreading the gospel and providing a place of worship for all.

Our Beliefs

We believe in the Holy Trinity, the divinity of Jesus Christ, and the power of the Holy Spirit. We follow the teachings and practices of the Celestial Church of Christ worldwide.

Our Mission

To provide spiritual guidance, promote Christian fellowship, and serve our community through worship, prayer, and outreach programs.
