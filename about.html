About Our Church

Our history, mission, and beliefs

Our History

The Celestial Church of Christ Redemption Parish in Winnipeg was established to serve the spiritual needs of the Celestial Church community in Manitoba. As part of the worldwide Celestial Church of Christ, we continue the rich tradition of worship and fellowship that began with the founding of the church.

Our Beliefs

We believe in the Holy Trinity - God the Father, God the Son (Jesus Christ), and God the Holy Spirit. We uphold the divinity of Jesus Christ, the power of the Holy Spirit, and the authority of the Bible as God's word. We follow the teachings and practices of the Celestial Church of Christ worldwide, emphasizing prayer, holiness, and evangelism.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide spiritual guidance, promote Christian fellowship, and serve our community through worship, prayer, and outreach programs. We aim to be a beacon of hope and a place of refuge for all seeking spiritual growth and connection with God.

Our Vision

To build a vibrant, growing community of believers who worship God in spirit and truth, serve one another in love, and reach out to our city with the message of salvation through Jesus Christ.

Core Values

Prayer

We believe in the power of prayer and maintain a strong prayer life individually and corporately.

Community

We foster a supportive community where members care for one another and grow together in faith.

Scripture

We base our teachings and practices on the Holy Bible as the inspired word of God.

Love

We demonstrate God's love through our actions, words, and service to others.
