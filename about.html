Sacred Foundations

Our history, divine mission, and celestial beliefs that guide our spiritual journey

Our Sacred History

The Celestial Church of Christ Redemption Parish in Winnipeg was divinely established to serve the spiritual needs of the Celestial Church community in Manitoba. As part of the worldwide Celestial Church of Christ, we continue the rich tradition of worship and fellowship that began with the founding of the church.

Our journey is one of faith, growth, and divine guidance, building a sanctuary where souls can connect with the celestial presence of God.

2005

Establishment of Redemption Parish in Winnipeg

2010

Acquisition of our current sacred ground

2018

Major sanctuary renovation and expansion

2024

Continued spiritual growth and community outreach

Our Divine Beliefs

We believe in the Holy Trinity - God the Father, God the Son (Jesus Christ), and God the Holy Spirit. We uphold the divinity of Jesus Christ, the power of the Holy Spirit, and the authority of the Bible as God's revealed word.

We follow the sacred teachings and practices of the Celestial Church of Christ worldwide, emphasizing prayer, holiness, evangelism, and the manifestation of God's power in our lives.

Our Divine Mission

Our mission is to provide spiritual guidance, promote Christian fellowship, and serve our community through worship, prayer, and outreach programs. We aim to be a beacon of hope and a place of refuge for all seeking spiritual growth and connection with God.

We strive to create an environment where every soul can experience divine transformation and find their purpose in God's grand design.

Our Celestial Vision

To build a vibrant, growing community of believers who worship God in spirit and truth, serve one another in love, and reach out to our city with the message of salvation through Jesus Christ.

We envision a parish where divine encounters are common, spiritual gifts are manifested, and lives are continually transformed by God's grace.

Our Sacred Core Values

Divine Prayer

We believe in the power of prayer and maintain a strong prayer life individually and corporately, seeking God's presence continually.

Sacred Community

We foster a supportive community where members care for one another and grow together in faith, love, and spiritual maturity.

Scriptural Foundation

We base our teachings and practices on the Holy Bible as the inspired word of God, our ultimate authority in faith and life.

Divine Love

We demonstrate God's love through our actions, words, and service to others, reflecting Christ's compassion in everything we do.
